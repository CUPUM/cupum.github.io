A new collaborator at the UNESCO Chair in Urban Landscape and a Master’s student in Urban Planning, Jennifer is conducting her research under the supervision of Shin Koseki. Her work focuses on the social impacts of artificial intelligence in citizen participation processes related to urban planning.
