Discover the article “Can AI make cities more inclusive? Yes, if communities remain in control”, by Rashid Muskani and Shin Koseki, published in The Conversation.

The article explores the potential of artificial intelligence to document and plan urban inclusivity. But one question remains: can it truly contribute without overshadowing people’s lived experiences behind models that turn our streets into mere scores on a map?
