We are proud to share that Eddy Nono has received the Graduate and Postdoctoral Scholarship (ESP) from the Université de Montréal. This award recognizes and supports master’s and doctoral students who stand out for the excellence of their academic record.

Beyond academic performance, this scholarship also highlights skills, personal qualities, life paths, and commitments that reflect the diversity of student profiles.

A collaborator with the chair for the past year, Eddy has contributed to many projects and workshops related to urban planning and artificial intelligence.
His master’s research focuses on citizen participation in policies for integrating AI into urban mobility in the Greater Montreal area. He has recently completed data analysis and interpretation of the results.

We look forward to sharing his research findings with you!
