Majdi Hunter-Batal, a collaborator with the Chair and a master's student in urban planning, is currently completing an internship at Enclume.

Focused on practical experience, this internship allows him to apply the knowledge and theories acquired throughout his studies. His mandate focuses on housing issues and aims to support and equip municipalities with the tools they need to address the housing crisis through urban consolidation strategies.

Throughout his internship, Majdi is developing key skills in mapping, knowledge mobilization, and project organization.

We wish him every success in this promising experience!
