Discover our latest episode, “Urban Mobility: AI in Motion,” hosted by Leandry Jieutsa and featuring Eddy Nono, collaborator at the UNESCO Chair in Urban Landscape.

In this episode, we discuss the key role of urban mobility in shaping today’s cities and how artificial intelligence and digital tools can help create more inclusive and accessible ways to move around.
It’s a rich conversation that opens the discussion and invites us to rethink the future of mobility together.
