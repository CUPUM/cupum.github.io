Episode 2 is now live!
For this episode, “Designing the City - Between Social and Digital”, Leandry Jieutsa welcomes Shin Koseki, Director and Chairholder of the UNESCO Chair in Urban Landscape, as well as Assistant Professor at the School of Urban Planning and Landscape Architecture in the Faculty of Environmental Design at the University of Montreal. His work focuses on designing responsible artificial intelligence systems for cities.
