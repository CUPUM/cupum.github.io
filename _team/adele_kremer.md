After obtaining her degree in Geography/Planning at the University of Paris 8, Adèle became interested in gender, identity spaces and the notion of inclusion. Within her masters in planning, option City, Territory, Landscape, she intends to use the multidisciplinary nature of the program to explore the appropriation spaces of homeless women in the urban context at night.
